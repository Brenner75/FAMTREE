Saemmer Elsa was born in the zodiac sign of Aries on a Thursday, the 21.03.1912 in Muenchen as daughter of
  • Mr. Maier, born in 1892 from Muenchen
  • and Mrs. (?), born in 1893 of Muenchen
Muenchen is the capital of Bavaria, Germany.

She had a sister Rosi. She was married to Saemmer Ludwig Raimund -, about 2 years older -, born in 1910 from Schwaz. She was a_house wife. They had a only daughter Liselotte, born in 1930.

She was wed in second marriage to Meier Jakob, a_driving instructor.

In addition to her sister Rosi she had 3 other sisters, but little is known about them. It was said that she was the most beautiful of the sisters.

She died at the age of 74 years in the year 1986 in Muenchen. Mourning were amongst others her daughter Liselotte Tschech, her granddaughter Ulrike Goedel, her great grandchild Michael Goedel.

She lived in the epoch of modern times and as a young girl under the reign of the Emperor Karl I of Habsburg-Lothringen. Some of her contemporaries of a similar age are Astrid Lindgren and Mutter Theresa. During her life there were many wars, amongst them the Great war, the Second World war and the Korean war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during her time are the 'Goldbaerchen' sweet, trains on magnetic rails, the jet engine and the helicopter.
