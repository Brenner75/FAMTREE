At that time he was already married to his wife Franziska, whom he had met in the Boy Scouts, and also had a two-year-old daughter and a 3 month old son.

At the beginning of 1975 the whole family emigrated to England, where they settled in Poole on the south coast. He worked briefly on an international project at the English Atomic Energy Authority, then in HTMP, a team of researchers, and finally started their his own business together with Franziska with a company that developed IT programs. The whole family was in the Scouts and would have stayed in England forever if it hadn't been for the great economic crisis at the end of the 80s, which drove his company into bankruptcy. He lost everything they owned including house and cars and had to leave England with the family as an economic refugee. Only his daughter married Joe - whom she had of course met in the scouts - stayed in England and gave him 3 wonderful grandchildren over the years.

He regained a foothold in Muenchen at the IBB company, but lost his wife a short time later to a serious cancer.
