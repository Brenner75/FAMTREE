  • Mock Thomas, born in 1782, a_watch maker from Wien III

Wien (Vienna) is the capital of Austria and you may look at some photos here.

She had a brother Johann Baptist, 1 years and 2 months younger, born in 1843. The father died when little Josefa was 4 years old. She was married to Mr. Ponfikel -, about 4 years older -, born in 1838.

The exact date of death is not known, but she died propably at the age of 67 years in the year 1909.

She lived in the epoch of Historicism and as a young girl under the reign of the Emperor Franz-Joseph I of Habsburg-Lothringen. During her life there were many wars, amongst them the Sardinian-Austrian war, the Hungarian Revolution and the Sardinian war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during her time are the raw material for the paper production, X-rays, the Aspirin pills, and the spark plug.
