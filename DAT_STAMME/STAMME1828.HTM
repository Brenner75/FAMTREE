The exact date of birth is not known, but (?) Stefanie was propably born 1916 in Boehmen. Boehmen Bohemia (Boehmen) is a part of the Check Republic.

She married in the year 1941 in einer_Ferntrauung Hawelka Willi - about the same age -, born in 1916 from Wien. About 1 years later had her daughter Stefanie, born in 1942.

After her husband was killed in Stalingrad and she had a one-year-old daughter, she had several love affairs in the soldiers' milieu, including an American who always promised to take her to America. In the end, however, he left her. Nobody knows what became of her later.

The exact date of death is not known, but she died propably at the age of 80 years in the year 1996.

She lived in the epoch of modern times and as a young girl under the reign of the Emperor Karl I of Habsburg-Lothringen. Some of her contemporaries of a similar age are Astrid Lindgren and Mutter Theresa. During her life there were many wars, amongst them the Second World war, the Korean war and the Vietnam war. Amongst important discoveries and inventions during her time are the 'Goldbaerchen' sweet, the jet engine, the helicopter and the nuclear fission.
