  • Zobl Johann, born in 1721 from Prutz
  • and Zegg Rosina, born in 1726 of Vals
Vals in Tirol is a village in a small valley near the 'Brennerpass' in Tyrol.

He married propably in the year 1779 Tringg Ursula -, 5 years younger -, born in 1760 from Vals. About 6 years later had their son Simon, born in 1785.

The exact date of death is not known, but he died propably at the age of 51 years in the year 1800.

He lived in the epoch of Rococo and as a young fellow under the reign of the Emperor Franz I. Stephan of Lothringenen. Some of his contemporaries of a similar age are Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart and Friedrich Schiller. During his life there were the Seven-Year war, Napoleon's wars and the Coalition wars.
